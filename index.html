About Shanah

Shanah (Shanitah) is a multidisciplinary designer and developer based in Kampala, Uganda. She specializes in creating clean, immersive web experiences that balance aesthetics with purposeful interactivity.

Her work merges thoughtful UI/UX, creative coding, and 3D animation — always with an emphasis on simplicity, elegance, and emotional impact.
